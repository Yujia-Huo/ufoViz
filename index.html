UFOs in the USA: Mapping Decades of sightings with Location, Trends, Shapes, and Witness Testimonies

UFO, or Unidentified Flying Object, was first mentioned by Kenneth Arnold in 1947. Since then, public interet about UFO sighting has dramatical increase. Also with the development of aerospace, we start to wondering if there actually other living creature in the space.

In the Very beginning, UFO sighting is more like a folklore and government's identify most of those sighting are just air ballon or firworks. However, the significant constant increase of sighting raise government's attention and admitted that there are certain percentage of the sighting are unidentified.

The following visualization will be based on dataset from National UFO Reporting Center to explore the UFO sighting to presenting different perspectives.

When look into the trending, it's a clear raise of UFO sighting in each year. But one interesting observation is that there is a big drop from 2014 to 2018, which need further study to explain this drop. And when take a look into the dsitribution of the location, it is noticable that sighting reports are more likely to cluster around major cities and has close relationship to the population density. The assumption can be people in the major cities has more chance to expose to various source of light, and has more access to the internet.
